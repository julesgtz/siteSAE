Why I chose this topic

I chose music because I listen to it all days,
I can't do without it, as soon as I work, that I have nothing
to do, I listen to music .So it was natural for me to talk about
that. I like music but especially rap, this style of music is the
style I prefer. The speed of the melodies, the instrumentals behind the lyrics. Moreover, rappers use lyrics to denounce,
say what they are going through, put it out there for all to see
what life is like, most of the time, in the suburbs
It's not always fun to live there.
It is therefore for all these reasons that
I chose rap.
